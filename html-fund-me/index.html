Welcome to Krishna Trust

At Krishna Trust, we are committed to ensuring that every tourist visiting the holy city of Mathura receives the best hospitality. We offer free accommodation, including food (breakfast and dinner), and shelter to those in need. Additionally, we provide guidance and support to help you explore the spiritual and cultural richness of Mathura.

Our mission is to make your stay in Mathura comfortable and spiritually fulfilling. Whether you are here for a pilgrimage or simply to experience the beauty of the city, Krishna Trust is here to serve you.

Join us, and let us take care of all your accommodation needs so that you can focus on your journey and connection with the divine.

Support Our Mission

Krishna Trust operates solely through the generous donations of kind-hearted individuals like you. Your contribution, no matter how small, helps us continue providing free accommodation, food, and guidance to tourists and pilgrims in Mathura. We invite you to support our mission and help us make a difference in the lives of those who visit this holy city.
